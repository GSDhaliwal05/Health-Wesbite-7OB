"Healthy Meal Plan - Canada’s Food Guide"

Meal Plan (Canada’s Food Guide)

Creating a balanced meal plan is important to ensure you are eating nutritious meals that align with your body's needs. According to Canada's Food Guide, your meals should include a variety of food groups: fruits and vegetables, whole grains, and proteins, including plant-based sources.

Example Meal Plan (for a day):

  • Breakfast: Rice.
  • Lunch: Rice.
  • Dinner: Roti.
  • Snack: Gornola Bar and Non Fat Jelly.

This meal plan provides a balanced mix of nutrients to keep your body energized and healthy throughout the day.

How to Make a Healthy Meal Plan

When making a healthy meal plan, consider the following steps:

  1. Choose a variety of foods from all the food groups to ensure a balanced intake of nutrients.
  2. Plan for meals that include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and protein sources like lean meats, legumes, and plant-based proteins.
  3. Be mindful of portion sizes and try to balance your calorie intake with your activity levels.
  4. Incorporate snacks that are nutrient-dense, such as fruits, nuts, and yogurt, to keep you energized.

By planning meals in advance, you can make better choices and stay on track with healthy eating habits.
