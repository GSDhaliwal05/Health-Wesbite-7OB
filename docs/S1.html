"About Healthy Eating"

What is Healthy Eating?

Healthy eating means consuming a variety of foods that provide the nutrients you need to maintain your health, feel good, and have energy. This includes a balance of fruits, vegetables, proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats.

What are some benefits of eating healthy?

Eating healthy can help prevent chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and obesity. It also improves your mental well-being, increases energy, supports a healthy immune system, and helps maintain a healthy weight.

What does the Healthy Eating plate look like? Explain each section and why we need it.

The Healthy Eating Plate divides the plate into sections for fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and protein. Each section is important for providing the necessary vitamins, minerals, and energy. Vegetables are rich in fiber, fruits provide essential vitamins, whole grains offer sustained energy, and protein supports muscle growth and repair.

What will happen if you don’t eat healthy, and why is it important?

If you don’t eat healthy, you increase your risk of developing chronic conditions like obesity, heart disease, and diabetes. Poor nutrition can also affect your mood, energy levels, and cognitive function, making it harder to perform daily tasks and enjoy life.

Why do we need micro and macronutrients?

Macronutrients (carbohydrates, proteins, and fats) are needed in large amounts because they provide energy. Micronutrients (vitamins and minerals) are needed in smaller amounts but are essential for many body functions, including immune health, bone health, and proper metabolism.
