"More about Healthy Eating"

What are unhealthy foods? Why are they classified as unhealthy? What impact do these foods have on our bodies?

Unhealthy foods are those that are high in sugars, unhealthy fats, and sodium while lacking essential nutrients. These foods are classified as unhealthy because they can lead to obesity, heart disease, and diabetes. They also cause inflammation and disrupt metabolism, leading to long-term health issues.

How do medical, social, emotional, environmental, and cultural factors affect our choice of foods?

Medical factors like allergies or dietary restrictions, social influences like family and friends, emotional factors such as stress eating, environmental factors like access to healthy food, and cultural influences all affect our food choices. These factors can either promote or hinder a healthy eating habit, depending on how they influence decisions.

What are some benefits of planning what you eat?

Planning meals helps ensure that you eat a balanced diet, control portion sizes, save time, reduce food waste, and manage your budget. It can also help you make healthier choices and avoid last-minute unhealthy decisions.
